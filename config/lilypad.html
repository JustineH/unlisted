Residents of Chinese origins comprise the majority of the area. The median family income for Collingwood VE is average compared to the rest of Vancouver. Here, you'll find that most people often drive to get around. The trip to work takes approximately 30 minutes.

However, the elementary school with the highest rating in the community is Vancouver College (rated 10), which may be worth the distance. The drive there is about 19 minutes.
